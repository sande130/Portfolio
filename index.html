About Me

Formerly a character designer in the animation industry, I became interested in web/graphic design in 2012 and fell in love with it. I still love illustration and cartooning (and will never stop!), but my focus for right now is on web development.

My main skill set resides in digital illustration, but I pride myself in being versatile in numerous mediums and software. I hope to improve and develop my web design skills to match my competence as a draftsman.
